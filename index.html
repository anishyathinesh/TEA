a bit about us

We are a group of high school students who are passionate about supporting Tamils affected by the Sri Lankan Civil War. Based in Washington State, we do our part to spread the news about Sri Lankan Tamils in our community.

At Tamil Eelam Awareness, we aim to:

  • communicate the effects of the SL civil war to the public
  • create and fundraise for projects that aid Tamils affected by the civil war
  • encourage remembrance of the thousands of Tamil lives lost
